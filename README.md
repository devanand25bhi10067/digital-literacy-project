Project Overview 

This project is based on the role of a Student pro Digital Ambassador, where the objective is to help peers understand and navigate the digital world effectively. The project focuses on building essential digital literacy skills such as creating a professional online presence, practicing safe internet usage, understanding digital communication, and being aware of cyber threats. 

The project consists of five modules, each designed to develop a specific aspect of digital literacy. All tasks are organized within this GitHub repository, along with supporting files, screenshots, and a detailed project report. Through this project, I gained practical experience using various online platforms and improved my understanding of professional digital behaviour, online safety, and ethical use of technology. 

 

Module Summaries 

Module 1: Digital Literacy Infographic 

In this module, I created an informative and visually appealing infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance in today’s digital age. The infographic highlights key aspects such as safe internet practices, responsible social media usage, digital footprints, and cybersecurity awareness. I focused on organizing information in a clear and concise manner so that it can be easily understood by a wide audience. This activity enhanced my creativity, design thinking, and ability to communicate complex ideas in a simplified visual format. 

Module 2: Digital Portfolio 

In this module, I developed my professional online presence using platforms like GitHub,LinkedIn and kaggle. I created and updated my profiles by adding personal details, academic achievements, skills, and project work. I also learned how to structure a portfolio effectively to make it attractive and informative for recruiters and professionals. This module helped me understand the importance of maintaining a strong digital identity and how it can impact future career opportunities. 

Module 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms 

In this module, I explored coding and collaboration platforms such as Hacker Rank and Google Forms. I practiced solving programming problems to strengthen my logical thinking and coding abilities. Additionally, I created a digital literacy quiz using Google Forms, which involved designing questions, structuring the form, and sharing it with others. This module improved my understanding of collaborative tools, online assessments, and the importance of continuous skill development in the field of technology. 

Module 4: Email Etiquette & Communication 

In this module, I learned the importance of professional communication in the digital environment. I drafted formal emails for academic and professional purposes, focusing on proper structure, tone, and clarity. I also created a checklist of social media dos and don’ts to understand appropriate online behaviour. This module helped me improve my communication skills and taught me how to present myself professionally in digital interactions. 

Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness 

In this module, I studied real-world cybercrime cases and analysed different types of cyber threats such as phishing, identity theft, hacking, and online fraud. I created a prevention checklist that outlines best practices for staying safe online, including password security, awareness of suspicious links, and safe browsing habits. This module enhanced my understanding of cybersecurity concepts and emphasized the importance of being cautious and responsible while using digital platforms.
